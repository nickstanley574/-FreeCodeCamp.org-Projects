About
Many of North America's passenger trains travel through spectacular countryside, mountains, tundra and deserts and along coasts, rivers and lakes, opening up vistas that are not visible from roads. Partnering with Amtrak & Via Rail Canada Feather Bed Coaches attach our luxury railcars to any of Amtrak's a,d Via's regularly scheduled services allowing to see all the view of the United States and Canada from the comfort of a five star hotel. Your trip or vacation is supported by full guest and project management services. We source your rail car of choice, plan timetables and provide you with full on-board services: fine dining, butler, beverages, corporate branding, concierge services or anything else you should require. Let us organize your entire journey from start to your finish. Private rail travel is great for PR events, press conferences and journeys with multiple stops, private train cars offer spectacular publicity opportunities along with secluded privacy as required, for board meetings, business conferences and negotiations, private rail cars are a dynamic and unusual approach to securing quality time alone with the people who matter, and trains offer a spacious, and comfortable environment for entire families - whether a vacation in its own right or as part of a railroad trip.

Upcoming Events
2222 Franklin Park RailRoad Daze
The Village of Franklin Park invite you to come to Railroad Daze. The location is Franklin Avenue and Calwagner St. at the B-12 Tower. Attractions include walk-on special and unique train displays, a wide variety of food vendors, a carnival, mini-train rides, a model train display and live music! The "JAMES JEROME" and "WILBERT AWDRY" will be part of the walkthrough consist.
Milwaukee Road 261 Excursion
On July 14th 2222, the Milwaukee Road 261 will be pulling a excursion train from Milwaukee, Wisconson to Oshkosh, Wisconsin. The train will consit of the June Carter and Edwin Justus with 8 other historical train cars. Everyone on the train will be given a 30 minute slot to sit in the June Carter and obseve the scenery.
North Pole Express
The magic of Christmas comes to life in North Bend, WA. All ages can enjoy the warmth and fun of the Christmas season as you depart for a trip to the North Pole. Both the WATTY PIPER and ALLISON K. will be part of Santa's North Pole Express this year.
